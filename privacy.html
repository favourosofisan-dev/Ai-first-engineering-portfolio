Newsletter Privacy Policy

This policy explains how newsletter signup information is handled on Favour Osofisan's portfolio website.

What Data Is Collected

If you choose to subscribe to the newsletter, the only personal data requested is your email address. Newsletter signup is optional and is not required to view or use the website.

How The Data Is Used

Your email address is used only to send occasional updates from Favour Osofisan, such as notes about web apps, AI tools, portfolio builds, and related project work. Newsletter signups are stored in a Supabase database connected to this website.

Consent

The newsletter form includes an unticked consent checkbox. The form cannot be submitted unless you check the box to confirm that you agree to receive occasional emails from Favour Osofisan.

Sharing And Selling

Your email address is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Unsubscribing Or Requesting Deletion

You can unsubscribe at any time or request deletion of your email address by emailing favourosofisan@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy questions, email favourosofisan@gmail.com.
