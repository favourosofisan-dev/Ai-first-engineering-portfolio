How I Work

I ship production-ready websites and web apps - fast, clean, and repeatable. AI assists me at every stage, but I never let it replace judgment. I use it to move faster, not to cut corners.

My workflow

  1. 01

    Define

    Clarify the audience, core pages, user flows, and what the product must achieve.

  2. 02

    Prototype & refine

    Turn the idea into a usable interface, test the flow, and refine the experience before scaling.

  3. 03

    Build & integrate

    Connect frontend, backend, auth, data, ecommerce, and AI features where they add real value.

  4. 04

    Ship & improve

    Deploy, review real usage, and improve the features that matter most.

AI-assisted, human-directed

I use AI to generate boilerplate, refactor code, and write documentation - cutting development time significantly.

I review every diff, fix what the AI misses, and maintain full control over architecture and user experience.

The result: faster delivery without sacrificing quality or clarity.
